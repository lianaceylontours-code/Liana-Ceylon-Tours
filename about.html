About Liana Ceylon Tours

Your trusted travel partner in exploring the wonders of Sri Lanka

Our Story

Liana Ceylon Tours was founded with a passion for showcasing the incredible beauty and rich culture of Sri Lanka. What started as a small local operation has grown into a trusted travel company that has helped thousands of visitors discover the magic of this island paradise.

Our team of experienced guides and travel experts are dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences that go beyond the typical tourist attractions. We believe in sustainable tourism that benefits local communities while preserving the natural and cultural heritage of Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

To provide authentic, personalized travel experiences that connect visitors with the heart and soul of Sri Lanka, while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit both travelers and local communities.
