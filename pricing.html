Frequently Asked Questions

What is not included in the tour price?

International flights, visa fees, travel insurance, personal expenses, alcoholic beverages, and tips for guides and drivers are not included in our tour packages.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized itineraries. Contact us with your preferences, and we'll design a tour that matches your interests and budget.

What is the group size?

Our standard group tours have a maximum of 12 people to ensure personalized attention. We also offer private tours for individuals, couples, and families.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a full refund for cancellations made at least 30 days before the tour start date. Between 15-30 days, we refund 50%. Less than 15 days is non-refundable.
